Garlic Toast Recipe

A staple in my household with Lasagna is Garlic Toast. The crunch from the toast is perfect alongside your Italian compatriot.

Ingredients

  • 1 loaf of bread
  • 3 cloves crushed garlic
  • 5 tsp butter
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp oregano

Instructions

  1. Set an oven rack about 6 inches from the heat source and preheat the oven's broiler. Cut loaf into ten 1-inch slices.
  2. Mix butter, garlic, oil, oregano, salt, and pepper together in a bowl; spread butter mixture on one side of each slice of bread; arrange bread slices, butter-side up, in a single layer on a baking sheet.
  3. Cook under the preheated broiler until slightly brown, checking frequently so they do not burn, about 5 minutes.
  4. Remove from oven and serve hot.
