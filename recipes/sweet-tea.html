Sweet Tea Recipe

To round out our recipes we have the ultimate thirst quencher, Sweet Tea. This Southern Sweet Tea recipe will help you beat the heat during those hot, humid days.

Ingredients (Makes 1 gallon)

  • 12 individual black tea bags
  • 1 cup sugar
  • Ice
  • Lemon slices

Instructions

  1. Bring 4 cups of the water to a boil in a small saucepan over medium-high heat, then remove from heat.
  2. After 5 minutes, remove the bags and discard.
  3. Add the sugar and stir until completely dissolved.
  4. Pour the tea base into a 1-gallon pitcher. Add 3 more quarts of water to the tea base to make a gallon of sweet tea and stir to combine.
  5. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or, ideally, overnight. Serve over ice with lemon slices.
